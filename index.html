About Us

Humbly serving you since 2011, The Atlas is a specialty coffee and tea house located at Walla Walla University and operated by its students. Our focus is crafting some of the highest quality coffee and tea beverages available in the Walla Walla Valley while maintaining a rustic, community based atmosphere. All are invited to read, chat, collaborate, or simply enjoy the space; perfect for a long haul study session or a quick drink with a friend.
